Ishan Motors is Polonnaruwa's premier authorized dealer for David Pieris Motor Company (DPMC) — the sole authorized importer of Bajaj vehicles in Sri Lanka. We specialize in Bajaj motorcycles, three-wheelers, and the innovative QUTE micro-car.

Our reputation is built on one promise: every vehicle leaves our workshop serviced to manufacturer standards, using only genuine Bajaj spare parts. No shortcuts. No compromises.

Beyond the main workshop, Ishan Motors Cushion House offers dedicated specialist interior comfort work — crafting and restoring seats and upholstery for bikes and three-wheelers.
